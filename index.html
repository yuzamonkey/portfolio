About me

I'm a full-time student at the University of Helsinki. Currently my major is computer science, after studying mathematics as major for two years. I will complete my bachelor's degree in the autumn semester of 2022. After that, I will continue directly to master's studies. In addition, I study part-time at the Sibelius Academy, majoring in viola.


My main focus is to advance in programming. Recently I've worked with TypeScript, React and GraphQL. I expect to use web-development tools a lot in the upcoming years, while taking steps to learn and use tools built for AI. What I've noticed from my projects so far, apart from the programming languages and libraries, is a theme for culture and music. These subjects seemingly interest me the most, and my future ideas with AI are also about music reading and sound recognition.


Below, I have some programming projects I've made so far, and a few memorable moments from my music career.


Programming

Half Eaten Biscuit

Half Eaten Biscuit is a web app targeted for artists to connect and show their work as individuals and groups. TypeScript is used to write the code. The main tools are React, GraphQL, MongoDB, Jest and Cypress.

Scorewriter

A small program to write, save and play a single line of staff. The user can select between 2 clefs, 3 key signatures and 4 time signatures. The code was written in Python and Tkinter-module was used for GUI.

Event calendar

A simple web app to create ads for events. The code was written in Python and the main tools were Flask and PostgreSQL.

Music

Conducting

During my time in the military service in the Conscript Band of the Finnish Defence Forces, I got my first touches of conducting an orchestra during the NCO-course. After the intensive eight week period, I was awarded a stipend for my success.

Studio recordings

Studio recordings have given me a chance to play multiple genres of music from mainstream summer pop to military marches. My most memorable recordings have been for a movie Eila, Rampe ja Likka and this relaxing monday evening.

Chamber music

Playing in small ensembles is the best. With different groups it's various types of music and different dynamic between the members. Some memorable events for me have been playing in castles, singing in a choir competition and taking part in international courses.
